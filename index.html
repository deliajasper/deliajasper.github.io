About Me

Engineer & Art Lover

Hello! I'm Delia, I'm currently a student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) double majoring in Electrical & Computer Engineering and Robotics Engineering. I've been around technology my entire life and fell in love with computers at a young age. I have also been doing theatre for almost 15 years, starting at age 6, and never stopping. I primarily stage manage, but have also done lighting design, producing, and just about anything else you can think of at least once.

My engineering experience is broad, as my robotics engineering degree has deepened my knowledge of Mechanical Engineering, Electrical & Computer Engineering, and Computer Science. Through my Electrical & Computer Engineering major, I have truly found my niche in embedded systems and low-level programming. I plan to expand my knowledge in PCB design and power systems as well.

I am looking forward to entering industry and working in a field that allows for my technical and non-technical skills to stand out. I love the fast-paced, collaborative environment of theatre, and it has taught me how to handle large scale projects and manage teams of over 50 people. I hope to find a role that allows me to combine my passion for engineering while still allowing me to be creative.
