About Me

// bio.md

I'm Delia, a student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute double majoring in Electrical & Computer Engineering and Robotics Engineering. I've been around technology my entire life and fell in love with computers at a young age.

My engineering work spans embedded systems, robotics, and low-level programming. My Robotics Engineering degree has deepened my knowledge across mechanical, electrical, and software domains — and I'm actively expanding into PCB design and power systems.

I've been doing theatre for almost 15 years, starting at age 6. I primarily stage manage — which means managing projects at 60+ people scale, coordinating interdisciplinary teams under tight deadlines. I also do lighting design and producing. Theatre has genuinely shaped how I think about systems, communication, and execution under pressure.

Currently, I am in the process of completing my undergraduate degree, and evaluating if graduate school is right for me. I'm looking for a future role where technical depth and collaborative leadership can coexist.
